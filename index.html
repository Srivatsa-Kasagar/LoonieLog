Capital Cost Allowance

Recognizes big purchases like computers, office furniture, and equipment that the CRA depreciates over time instead of deducting in full. Flags them for your accountant — never guesses.

Gift & gift card CRA rules

Three CRA buckets: client gifts, employee gifts, personal. AI flags all gift purchases for recipient classification — correct T2125 line suggested per bucket.

WFH / home office

Home office expenses require Part 7 % calculation, not a receipt total. LoonieLog detects WFH expenses and routes them to Needs Review with the exact CRA instruction.

Flags for manual % calculation

SHA-256 hash prevents double-processing the same receipt. Archive folder rolls to a new year each January automatically — no manual folder management needed.

FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need before you install.

Is it really free?
LoonieLog is free to download and use. You need a Google account (free) and a Gemini API key — which has a generous free tier. Claude costs a few cents per month at typical receipt volumes.
Does it work for all provinces?
Yes — all 13 provinces and territories, with verified 2026 rates. HST provinces, QST for Quebec, and GST-only provinces like Alberta and Yukon are all handled correctly.
What if the AI gets a category wrong?
Every row has a CRA Category dropdown you can change with one click. Low-confidence extractions are automatically routed to a "Needs Review" tab and highlighted.
What happens to my API key?
Your key is stored inside Google's secure, encrypted storage — the same infrastructure Google uses to protect your account settings. It never appears in your sheet and is never sent to any LoonieLog server (there isn't one).
Does it handle USD receipts?
Yes. It fetches the Bank of Canada noon rate for the receipt date and converts everything to CAD — the CRA-accepted method. Original USD amount and rate are stored in separate columns.
Can I use it for a corporation (T2)?
LoonieLog is optimized for sole proprietors filing T2125 with their T1 return. Corporate T2 filers have different requirements and a different filing structure.
How does it find my old receipts?
The Dashboard has a "Hunt Past Receipts" button that searches Gmail for the past 90 days using receipt keywords — catching everything you missed before installing.
Is it open source?
Yes — all code is visible directly inside your Google Sheet's script editor. You can read every line before you install. Nothing is hidden or obfuscated.
